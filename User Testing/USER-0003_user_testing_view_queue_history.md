## **USER-0003:** User testing - View Queue History
> **Summary:** Verify that a user can view their past queue transactions in the profile section. <br>

**Preconditions:** User must be logged in and have previously completed or cancelled queue sessions.

**Scenario 1**

| # | Step | Expected Behavior |
|---|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Navigate to the `Profile` screen. | Verify that the profile navigation tabs (`Personal`, `History`, `Security`) are visible. |
| 2 | Tap the `History` tab. | Verify that the `Queue History` section is shown. |
| 3 | Observe the history list. | Verify that previous queue entries (e.g., Canteen, Infirmary) are displayed with their respective dates and wait times. |

**Post-conditions:**
- The user can review their queue history logs.
